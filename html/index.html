What is Procrastity Belt?

Procrastity Belt is a Chrome-extension that assists you in avoiding distratctions on the web not related to the task at hand.

How does it work?

You enter a few URLs related to, for example, your history paper and Procrastity Belt scans the pages for reoccuring concepts. It then scans the pages you access to make sure they are relevant to what you're doing.

What API's do we use?

We use the Alchemy API to scan the pages for concepts.
